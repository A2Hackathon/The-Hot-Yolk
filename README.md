The Hot Yolk is the product made by us in Carnegie Mellon's NexHacks hackathon, utilising products from Overshoot, WisprFlow and many more.

Primary use: 
- Most game developers are either great engineers or great designers—not both. The Hot Yolk is an AI-powered dream world generator that lets developers create rich, playable worlds through simple prompts, turning imagination into game environments in minutes instead of months.
